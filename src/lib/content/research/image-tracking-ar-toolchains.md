## Question

For overlaying animation on a physical poster, which toolchain wins — and on
which axis (reach, tracking quality, dev experience, polish)?

## Contenders

- **8th Wall** — web, zero-install, broadest reach.
- **ARKit (iOS)** — native, strong tracking, app-store friction.
- **VisionOS** — best experience I've built, smallest audience.

## Axes to compare

- Image-tracking robustness (lighting, angle, distance)
- Install friction vs. fidelity tradeoff
- Animation playback quality on a tracked target

## Notes from the field

- Flowers show ran on 8th Wall for reach; VisionOS version was the standout.
