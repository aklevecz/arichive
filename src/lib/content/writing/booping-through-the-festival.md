## The hunt

Four fragments hidden across the festival grounds. Boop one with your phone, see
the next location appear on the map. Collect all four to complete the puzzle.

## The payoff

Once the set was complete, players viewed the puzzle through an AR lens to reveal
their own unique animation. Every fragment — and the final animation — was an NFT
minted on Base.

## The side quest

Scattered eggs with different point values. Most points won 0.5 ETH on Base.

## The tech

SvelteKit · 8th Wall · Cloudflare Workers · Solidity / Base · SDXL · Deforum · AnimateDiff.
