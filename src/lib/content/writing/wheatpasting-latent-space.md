## The arc

Generate → curate → animate → print → paste → augment.

- Hundreds of lilies in SDXL; 21 selected, upscaled, outpainted to poster ratio.
- Each flower run through Deforum for a trippy, immersive loop.
- Printed and wheatpasted onto wooden canvas boards — bringing latent space into the physical, organic world.

## Bringing them to life

- 8th Wall image tracking overlays the animation on the physical poster.
- A VisionOS/ARKit version that was the best experience by far — and still needs refining.

## Why physical matters

The wheatpaste texture, the imperfection, the wood grain. What does AI art gain
by being dragged out of the screen?
